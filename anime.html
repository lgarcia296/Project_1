My Favorite Anime

I first started watching anime during Covid-19, and ever since that time, I've watched more than 30 anime series. Here are my top 3 favorites.

#3

Naruto is one of my favorite anime, and it ranks third because I enjoy the fights and the story it tells. It also led me to explore other similar anime.

#2

Bleach is a captivating story centered on sword fighting. The animation and fight scenes are something I deeply enjoy, making it my second favorite.

#1

Yu-Gi-Oh takes the number one spot because I started watching and playing it in 4th grade. I still buy Yu-Gi-Oh cards and play to this day, keeping over 100 cards as a cherished memory.
